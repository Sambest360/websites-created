About Us:

Group 3

In an assignment given to us by our University Lecturer, Mr Femi we were given the task of solving a problem faced in the world we live in with a device known as Raspberry Pi. My group came up with the plan to create something known as a Virtual Library to provide easy access to kids who don't have access to a library in the area they live in.

What we provide?

We offer access points in areas who's school children do not have access to the internet for school use
